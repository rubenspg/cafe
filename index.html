Cafe Favela

When we were planing to open the cafe we wanted people to feel at home. The cafe, the events, the Brazilian products, snacks and drinks are just an excuse to connect the community. Our community is made of people who are connected to Brazil, people who are curious and excited about the Brazilian culture, Brazilians and everybody who is open to try something new - like you! Come to experience community and life celebrations - just like Brazilians!

Misto Quente

Misto quente is one of the most traditional sandwiches in Brazil: a grilled “Pão Francês” (French bread), with manteiga (butter), presunto e queijo prato (ham and cheese)!
